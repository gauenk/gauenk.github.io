Oh My Question!

Oh My Question! is a new experiential learning we (Prof Stanley Chan and his teaching team) design and implement for Spring 2024. In “Oh My Question!” you create problems, prepare solutions, and present your answers to your peers. The motivation is to improve the learning of students (i.e., you!!) by encouraging you to think about probability and random processes in daily life. Our goal is to fight against comments such as “this class is boring”. More details are available here.
