Grades

The grades will be curved. The breakdown is below:

  • Homework: 35%. Lowest HW score is dropped.

  • Midterm Exam 1: 20%

  • Midterm Exam 2: 20%

  • Final Exam: 25%

Exams

The midterms are scheduled for June 25th and July 16th from 8pm - 9pm EDT. If you live outside of the USA, you may take the exam on June 26th and July 17th from 8am - 9am EDT. Email Kent Gauen (gauenk@purdue.edu) directly to request the alternative exam time. Unless the circumstance is exceptional, we do not offer make-up exams.

The midterms use Examity. Please see Brightspace for more details.

Midterm 1 will test the topics colored blue. Midterm 2 will test the topics colored green. The Final Exam covers all course content.

Homework

Homework is posted to Gradescope and due Fridays at 11:59pm. Homework solutions are linked on the front page of Brightspace. Homework assignments are graded by randomly selecting a few problems (about three) and grading for correctness.

DRC

Please submit your requests through DRC. Once we recieve a confirmation, you will be granted extra time when taking the exam with everyone else.
